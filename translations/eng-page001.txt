There is no need to read the entire book from the beginning all at once.
You only need to read the section for the corresponding month or year at that specific time.
When your child turns one month old, read the one-month section; once they turn one year old, you only need to read the one-year section.
Through sections like "The Baby of This Month" and "The Child of This Year," I want you to understand in advance the outline of growth in each period and how individuality manifests.
I want you to observe these developments.
Even things that seem abnormal to a mother are often simply an expression of the child's individuality.
Under "Children's Illnesses," in addition to infectious diseases like "colds," conditions like "headaches" and "obesity" that often occur in elementary school children are also compiled for when they arise.
Furthermore, due to the organizational structure of this book, if you cannot find a particular abnormality, please consult the "Index." It may be included under sections such as "How to Raise," "Environment," or "Group Childcare."
